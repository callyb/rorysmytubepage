ESSENTIAL INFORMATION

Please note that the Website is a private site for Rory and his friends. 1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect a child's full name and email address, and a password so that they can change or delete their contact info if they want to. We also collect parent's name & email address for anyone under 13.


2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use a child's email address to send them updates when new videos are posted and occasional updates on 567turtle and his videos. We use parent's contact details to get permission from them for their child to receive updates. We use the password for the child or parent to change or delete their contact information any time they want to.


3. WILL YOUR INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH ANYONE?

We don't expect to share your information with anyone, and we will not be sharing our mailing list for any reason. We would only share information with your consent, to comply with the law, to provide you with services, or to protect your rights.


4. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?

We keep your information for as long as necessary to provide you with updates as shown in point 2, unless otherwise required by law. Once we are not doing this any more either because we have discontinued rorysmytube or because updates we send you no longer go through to the email address we have for you, then we will delete our record of you.


5. HOW DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION SAFE?

We aim to protect your personal information by organising our information and keeping it protected in a secure database provided by Google (Firebase). Even though we try very hard to make sure your information is protected, there is no storage that is 100% safe on the internet, so we cannot promise or guarantee that hackers, cybercriminals or other people will not be able to get around our security and collect, steal or change your information. Although we do not expect this to happen on rorysmytube, we have to tell you that you give us your information at your own risk. You should also only use our website or any website on a secure computer or device, and take any precautions you can to keep your connection secure.
